How to convert HEIC to JPG in under a minute

If someone says "I can’t open your iPhone photo," this is the quickest way to fix it.

On iPhone

  1. Tap Pick Photos.
  2. Keep format set to JPG.
  3. Tap Share.
  4. Choose Save Image or send in Messages/Mail right away.

On desktop

  1. Drag HEIC files into the drop area.
  2. Use JPG unless you have a specific reason to pick another format.
  3. Share small batches, or use Download ZIP for larger batches.

When should I use PNG, WebP, or PDF instead?

  • PNG: best for screenshots and crisp text.
  • WebP: best for smaller web uploads.
  • PDF: best for print or document submission.

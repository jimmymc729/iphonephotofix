    Quick Answers

    What is HEIC?

    HEIC is Apple’s image format. Some apps and websites do not accept it.

    Does this convert Live Photos?

    It converts the still image from a Live Photo. Motion/video is not included.

    How do I share converted photos?

    Tap Share if available. If not, tap Save, then send from Photos or Files.

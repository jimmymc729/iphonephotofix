How to upload iPhone photos to Google Classroom

If Classroom rejects your image, the photo is usually fine. The most common issue is file format: iPhone often uses HEIC, while upload flows work best with JPG or PNG.

Fastest way to submit (student or parent)

  1. Open iPhone Photo Fix.
  2. Select the photos you need for the assignment.
  3. Set output to JPG (or PNG if your teacher asked for it).
  4. Tap Share or Save, then save to Photos or Files.
  5. Attach the converted file in Google Classroom.

What to choose in Classroom

  • JPG: best default for most photo assignments.
  • PNG: better for screenshots or diagrams.
  • PDF: use when your teacher asks for one document upload.

If upload still fails

  • Try uploading one file first to confirm it works.
  • Keep file names simple (letters and numbers only).
  • Use smaller batches if your connection is slow.
  • If needed, use Download ZIP and re-upload from desktop.
